## About Me

I am currently a second year Ph.D. student at the [Institute of Automation](https://english.cas.cn/), [Chinese Academy of Sciences](https://english.cas.cn/), under the supervision of [Prof. Jinqiao Wang](http://www.nlpr.ia.ac.cn/iva/homepage/jqwang/index.htm). I work on continual/lifelong learning of computer vision and natural language processing models.

## Research Interests

- Computer Vision
- Continual/Lifelong Learning
- Vision and Language Models
